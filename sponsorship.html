Bronze Sponsors

Boston University's Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering is proud to sponsor the IEEE Young Professionals Summit 2026, hosted this year on our own campus at the BU Photonics Building. Tracing its roots to Alexander Graham Bell's work at BU, our department pairs a long history of innovation with more than $32 million in annual research spanning artificial intelligence and machine learning, integrated photonics and quantum systems, autonomous and secure systems, and neuroengineering — with a faculty that includes National Academy members and dozens of professional-society fellows. We care deeply about launching early-career engineers: our graduate and professional master's programs, including a new online MS in Software Engineering for Artificial Intelligence, together with hands-on research, industry partnerships, and mentorship, help engineers move confidently from the classroom into meaningful technical careers. We look forward to welcoming this year's young professionals to Boston.
