About the Summit

The IEEE Young Professionals (YP) Summit is an annual gathering designed to empower the next generation of IEEE leaders. This dynamic event provides a collaborative platform where young professionals come together to shape the future of technology and leadership.

This year, we focus specifically on the transformative role of technology with our theme: Towards Sustainable Artificial Intelligence. Join us in New York City to be part of impactful conversations and initiatives that will influence sustainable technological progress.
