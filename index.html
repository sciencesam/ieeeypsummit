About the Summit

The IEEE Young Professionals (YP) Summit is an annual gathering designed to empower the next generation of IEEE leaders. This dynamic event provides a collaborative platform where young professionals come together to shape the future of technology and leadership.

This year, we focus specifically on the transformative role of technology with our theme: Artificial Intelligence and the Future of Human Work. Join us in Boston to be part of impactful conversations and initiatives that will influence artificial intelligence and the future of human work.
