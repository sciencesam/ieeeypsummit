Program Schedule

The 2026 IEEE Young Professionals Summit will take place at Boston University Photonics Building (PHO).

Registration will open at 4:00 PM on Friday. The main program will continue through Saturday and Sunday.

The summit will feature multiple tracks, including:

  • Technical Innovation & Applied AI
  • AI in Careers & Professional Growth
  • Women in Engineering
