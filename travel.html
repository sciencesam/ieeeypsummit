Travel Costs

As an IEEE Young Professionals leader, there are potentially several sources of funding available to subsidize or cover your travel, lodging, and registration:

  • The $300 2025 IEEE Regions 1 and 2 Young Professionals Travel Grant
      • Contact us for more information
  • Your IEEE Section
    • Talk to your Section Chair and attend your Section ExCom to inquire
  • Other IEEE Organizational Units (e.g. your Chapter, Society)
  • Your Employer
If IEEE is funding your attendance, be sure to abide by the Expense Reimbursement Guidelines for IEEE Volunteers.

If you are coming to the YP Summit from outside the NYC metropolitan area, here are expected ranges of costs to attend:

How to Get to NYC

NYC is easily accessible from across IEEE Regions 1 and 2:

  • Direct flights to EWR, JFK, and LGA Airports
  • By Amtrack, Metro-North, NJ Transit, and LIRR
  • By Car
Regardless of how you get to NYC, we recommend using the NYC Subway and NJ Transit to get around. The NYC Subway runs 24/7 and now accepts contactless payment. If you are coming by car, try to park outside Manhattan either at your hotel, in Long Island City, or New Jersey; parking in Manhattan is expensive and Congestion Pricing is in effect below 60th street.

Where to Stay

We encourage attendees to book accommodations that best fit their preferences and budget. There is no official hotel block, and hotel accommodations are not included in Summit registration.

New York City and the surrounding metro area offer a wide range of hotel options with excellent public transit access to our venue at 60 Pine Street, NYC.

Many attendees choose to stay in New Jersey or outer boroughs of NYC to manage costs. The Summit schedule allows plenty of time for daily travel.
